MARVEL'S: IRON MAN

The man who saved half the universe

Iron Man's timeline:

  • 1970 - Born in Manhattan, NYC, son to Howard and Maria Stark
  • 1977 - Attended Phillips Academy in Andover, MA
  • 1986 - Wins 4th Annual MIT Robot Design Award
  • 1991 - Howard and Maria Stark died of assassination
  • 1992 - Takes over Obadiah Stane's role as CEO of Stark Industries
  • 2009 - Travels to Afghanistan to showcase a new wepon and gets kidnapped by the Ten Rings and builds the first iteration of his Iron Man armor
  • 2012 - He joined the Avengers together with Captain America, Hawkeye, Black Widow, Hulk, and Thor
  • 2015 - He created Ultron who killed Jarvis
  • 2016 - He fights Captain America in the Superheroe's Civil War
  • 2018 - Fights Thanos, and loses resulting in the disapperance of half the Universe's population
  • 2019 - Sacrifices himself to bring back half the universe

“Part of the journey is the end.” The journey symbolizes life – life has a beginning and the end and so even Tony’s journey must have an ending.

-- Tony Stark "Iron Man"
